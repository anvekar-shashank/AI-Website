The ECE Department is equipped with modern facilities, advanced laboratories, and comprehensive resources to provide an excellent learning environment for students.

Laboratory Facilities

Detailed Laboratory Information

1. Advanced Communication Lab

Room No.: 201  |  Carpet Area: 74 Sq. Mts

Major Equipment:

  • Cathode Ray Oscilloscopes, Digital Storage Oscilloscopes, Digital Multimeters
  • Function Generators, DC Regulated Power Supplies
  • Optical Fiber Trainer Kits (OFT Kits)
  • Microwave Bench Setup, Klystron Power Supply
  • Digital Communication Trainer Kits
  • Microwave Integrated Circuit (MIC) Setup

Focus Area: Digital and Analog Communication Techniques (ASK, FSK, PSK, DPSK, AM, FM, PAM); Microwave Communication (MIC Trainer) — X-Band (8.2–12.4 GHz), S-Band (2.2–3.3 GHz)

2. Analog Electronics Lab

Room No.: 205  |  Carpet Area: 74 Sq. Mts

Major Equipment:

  • CROs, DSOs, Digital Multimeters
  • Function Generators, DC Regulated Power Supplies (Single/Dual)
  • Power Modules, Power Scope
  • 3-Phase Star & Delta Circuit Modules; 3-Phase Power using Two Wattmeter
  • Earth Tester with Spikes, Demonstration Cut-out Section Module
  • Analog/AC/DC Voltmeters & Ammeters, Auto-transformer/Variac
  • HP NeverStop Laser 1000a

Focus Area: Linear Integrated Circuits, Op-amp Applications, Active Filters, Modulation basics

3. Digital Electronics Lab

Room No.: 202  |  Carpet Area: 74 Sq. Mts

Major Equipment:

  • IC Trainer Kits, IC Tester
  • Analog CROs
  • DC Regulated Power Supplies, Function Generators

Focus Area: Logic Circuit Design — Encoders, Decoders, Multiplexers, Counters

4. DSP Lab / Microprocessor Lab

Room No.: 235  |  Carpet Area: 74 Sq. Mts

Major Equipment:

  • Computer Systems (Intel Pentium G630 @ 2.7GHz)
  • Interfacing Modules
  • Analog CROs, DSOs
  • DC Fixed Power Supplies
  • Digital Signal Processing (DSP) Kits, Dot Matrix Printer
  • Trainer Kit, PCI Parallel Port Add-on Cards
  • MATLAB (25 User)
  • Microcontroller Kits, Interfacing Modules, Universal Programmer

Focus Area: MATLAB Programming, 8051 Microcontroller Programming, DSP experiments

5. Embedded Controller Lab / HDL Lab

Room No.: 236  |  Carpet Area: 149 Sq. Mts

Major Equipment:

  • ARM Cortex-M3 LPC1768 Evaluation Boards
  • Desktop Computers (Intel Pentium G630 @ 2.7GHz)
  • CROs 30 MHz, DSOs 70 MHz (1Gs/s)
  • DC Fixed Power Supply (+5V, +12V, -12V / 1A)
  • D-Link DES-1024D 24-Port LAN Hub
  • FPGA/CPLD Trainer Kits, Xilinx ISE 7.1
  • Logic Analyzer, Pattern Generator, Smart Board

Focus Area: HDL Programming (Xilinx), ARM Cortex Programming (LPC1768), Embedded C

6. VLSI Lab

Room No.: 237  |  Carpet Area: 149 Sq. Mts

Major Equipment / Software:

  • Desktop Computers (Intel Core i3-4170 @ 3.70 GHz; Intel Core i5-11400 @ 2.60 GHz)
  • LCD Projector
  • Tanner EDA Design Tool (20 User)
  • Xilinx ISE 14.7
  • Cadence 8.7 (30 User)

Focus Area: Schematic Design using Cadence (VLSI), 40/90/180 nm Technology
