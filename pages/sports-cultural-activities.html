Sports and Cultural Activities

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering emphasizes holistic student development by actively encouraging participation in sports and cultural activities alongside academics. Students regularly engage in intra- and inter-collegiate sports competitions, showcasing their talent in athletics, team games, and individual events that promote physical fitness, teamwork, leadership, and discipline.

Cultural activities such as technical fests, cultural festivals, talent shows, music, dance, drama, and creative arts provide students with a platform to express creativity, enhance confidence, and develop organisational and interpersonal skills. The department also motivates students to take part in university-level and national-level events, fostering a spirit of collaboration, innovation, and healthy competition.

These activities not only enrich campus life but also contribute to the overall personality development of students, helping them balance academic excellence with social, cultural, and emotional growth.

Student Clubs & Societies

  • Sports Club: Organizing sports activities and coaching sessions
  • Music Club: For music enthusiasts - instrumental, vocal, and band performances
  • Dance Club: Classical, contemporary, and hip-hop dance groups
  • Drama Club: Theater productions and street plays
  • Photography Club: Capturing campus life and organizing photo walks
  • Literary Club: Debates, writing, and public speaking activities

Celebrations & Festivals

  • Freshers' Welcome: Grand welcome for new students
  • Teachers' Day: Special programs honoring faculty
  • Engineers' Day: Celebrating engineering achievements
  • Independence Day & Republic Day: Patriotic celebrations
  • Cultural Festival Days: Diwali, Holi, Onam, and other festivals
  • Farewell: Memorable send-off for graduating students

Health & Wellness Programs

  • Yoga & Meditation: Daily morning sessions for mental and physical wellness
  • Fitness Programs: Regular workout sessions and fitness challenges
  • Health Check-ups: Periodic medical camps and health awareness
  • Stress Management: Workshops on managing academic pressure
