GMU ECE Learning Platform (LEAP)

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering provides comprehensive learning resources through the GM University Learning Enhancement and Advancement Platform (LEAP). This platform offers specialized courses and resources tailored for ECE students to enhance their technical skills and knowledge.

🚀 Explore LEAP Platform

Available Course Categories

Our LEAP platform organizes ECE content into specialized categories for comprehensive learning:

📚 Core ECE Subjects

Electronic Circuits & Devices
  • Semiconductor physics and diode applications
  • Transistor amplifiers and biasing
  • Operational amplifier circuits
  • Power electronics fundamentals
Digital Electronics & Logic Design
  • Boolean algebra and logic gates
  • Combinational and sequential circuits
  • Flip-flops and registers
  • Digital system design principles
Electromagnetic Field Theory
  • Static electric and magnetic fields
  • Maxwell's equations
  • Transmission lines and waveguides
  • Antenna fundamentals

📡 Communication Systems

Analog Communication
  • Amplitude and frequency modulation
  • AM/FM transmitter and receiver design
  • Noise analysis in communication systems
Digital Communication
  • PCM and digital modulation techniques
  • Error control coding
  • Information theory fundamentals
Wireless Communication
  • Mobile communication systems
  • Cellular network architecture
  • Wireless propagation characteristics

🔌 VLSI Design & Embedded Systems

VLSI Design
  • CMOS logic design
  • VHDL/Verilog programming
  • ASIC design flow
  • FPGA implementation
Embedded Systems
  • Microcontroller programming
  • Real-time operating systems
  • IoT system design
  • Embedded Linux applications

📊 Signal Processing

Digital Signal Processing
  • Discrete-time signals and systems
  • DFT and FFT algorithms
  • Digital filter design
  • Spectral analysis
Image & Speech Processing
  • Image enhancement and restoration
  • Pattern recognition
  • Speech signal analysis
  • Audio processing fundamentals

🎛️ Control Systems & Automation

Classical Control Theory
  • Transfer functions and block diagrams
  • Stability analysis
  • Root locus method
  • Bode plot analysis
Modern Control Systems
  • State-space representation
  • Optimal control theory
  • Digital control systems
  • Industrial automation
