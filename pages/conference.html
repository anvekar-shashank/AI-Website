Academic Conferences & Technical Workshops

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering actively organizes and participates in national and international conferences, workshops, and seminars to foster academic excellence, research collaboration, and knowledge sharing among students, faculty, and industry professionals.

NCSEEE 2025 – Inaugural Session Highlights

National Conference on Smart Systems and Emerging Trends in Electronics & Electrical Engineering (NCSEEE 2025)

Date: 21-08-2025 | Venue: GM University, Davanagere | Organized by: Departments of ECE & EEE
The conference was successfully inaugurated and featured two insightful keynote addresses.
  • Keynote Address 1: Ms. Mamatha G R, Deputy General Manager, Smart City, Davanagere
  • Keynote Address 2: Dr. Manjunath P, Principal, BLDE College of Engineering, Vijayapura
  • Papers Presented: 31 (ECE) and 20 (EEE)

NCSEEE 2025 provided a vibrant platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to exchange knowledge, present innovative research, and discuss future trends in smart systems, electronics, and electrical engineering.
