Memorandum of Understanding (MOUs)

Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs) serve as strategic collaborations between the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering and industries, research organizations, academic institutions, and professional bodies. These partnerships aim to bridge the gap between academia and industry by facilitating knowledge exchange, collaborative research, internships, industrial visits, joint workshops, and training programs.

MoUs provide students with opportunities to gain practical exposure to emerging technologies, access industry-relevant tools and expertise, and enhance their employability skills. They also promote faculty development, innovation, and entrepreneurship through joint projects and resource sharing. Ultimately, MoUs strengthen industry–academia linkages, enrich the learning ecosystem, and prepare students to meet global challenges in the ever-evolving field of electronics and communication.
