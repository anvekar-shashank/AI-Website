Alumni

The alumni of the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering are a strong pillar of support and inspiration for the department. Since its inception, the department has produced graduates who have established successful careers in core electronics industries, IT, telecommunications, research organizations, entrepreneurship, and higher studies in reputed institutions in India and abroad.

The alumni network serves as a valuable resource by contributing to departmental growth through knowledge sharing, guest lectures, mentoring, internships, and placement opportunities for current students. Alumni also play a key role in bridging the gap between industry and academia by providing insights into emerging trends, skill requirements, and career guidance.
