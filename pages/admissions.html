Join the ECE Department at GM University

Embark on an exciting journey in Electronics and Communication Engineering! The ECE Department at GM University offers a comprehensive B.Tech. program that prepares you for a successful career in the dynamic field of electronics and communication technology.

Program Offered

B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering

  • Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)
  • Intake: 120 seats
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • Entrance: Management Quota / CET Quota
  • KCET Code: E303

Eligibility Criteria

  • Admission to 1st year: Candidates passed in second PUC or equivalent and entrance exams like KCET etc.
  • Admission to 2nd year (Lateral Entry): Candidates who have passed a 3-year Diploma in Engineering.

Curriculum Based on Recent Trends

The program is continuously updated to incorporate emerging technologies such as Internet of Things (IoT), Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Embedded Systems, and VLSI Design, keeping students in sync with current industry demands.

Hands-On Laboratory Experience

Well-equipped laboratories and a strong emphasis on project-based learning provide students with practical exposure and technical proficiency.

Interdisciplinary Approach

The curriculum blends core concepts from electronics, computer science, and communication engineering to foster versatile problem-solving skills.

Skill Development & Certification

Students are encouraged to pursue relevant certifications and training programs in trending technologies to enhance employability.

Experienced Faculty with Research Focus

Faculty members actively engage in research, innovation, and consultancy, offering students mentorship and opportunities to participate in cutting-edge projects.

Internships & Industry Collaboration

Strong ties with industries and research institutions facilitate internships, industrial visits, and collaborative projects.

Alumni Network and Mentorship

Students benefit from guidance and inspiration through interactions with alumni placed in reputed organizations and higher education institutions globally.

Admission Process

  1. Fill online application form on university website
  2. Upload required documents and pay application fee
  3. Appear for entrance examination
  4. Check merit list and attend counseling
  5. Complete seat allotment and fee payment
  6. Submit original documents for verification

Documents Required

  • 10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates
  • Transfer and migration certificates
  • Entrance exam score card
  • Caste and income certificates (if applicable)
  • Aadhar card and photographs

Fee Structure

Invest in your career with our transparent and flexible payment options:

Management Quota

Annual Fee: ₹ 2,10,000

CET Quota

As decided by Government of Karnataka.

Scholarships & Waivers

  • 100% Tuition Fee Waiver for students securing ranks within the top 2000 in KCET.
  • 75% Tuition Fee Waiver for students with outstanding achievements in sports at the state or national level.
  • Scholarships for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds, including the Smt. Halamma and Shri. G. Mallikarjunappa Scholarship, which supports meritorious students from low-income families.

Career Opportunities

VLSI Design Engineer, Embedded Systems Developer, Communication Engineer, IoT Solutions Architect, Software Engineer, Network Engineer, R&D Engineer, Higher Studies (M.Tech./MS/Ph.D.), Entrepreneurship
