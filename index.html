Welcome to the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)

A vibrant center for innovation, learning, and research. Our four-year B.Tech. program provides a strong foundation in Analog and Digital Electronics, Microprocessors, Communication Systems, Signal Processing, VLSI Design, and Embedded Systems, preparing students to thrive in a rapidly evolving technological world. The curriculum blends core engineering concepts with emerging domains such as IoT, Wireless Communication, Artificial Intelligence in Electronics, and Automation, emphasizing experiential learning through modern laboratories, projects, and internships. At ECE, we nurture creativity, analytical thinking, and multidisciplinary collaboration, shaping future-ready engineers and innovators poised to lead in industry and research globally.

Department Vision, Mission & Objectives

🎯
Department Vision

Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) will have a transformative impact on society through continual innovation in Electronics and Communication Engineering education, research, skill development, creativity, and entrepreneurship.

🚀
Department Mission
  • To disseminate knowledge and conduct research in Electronics and Communication Engineering with learner centric approach
  • To teach skills such as critical thinking, creativity and innovation, collaboration, communication, technical and digital, flexibility and adaptability, cultural values, and leadership and responsibility
  • To develop global citizens by educating students on emotional, physical, social, economic, environmental, spiritual dimensions of human growth in addition to intellectual pursuits
  • To address real-world challenges and to establish the groundwork for entrepreneurship and lifelong learning
🎯
Department Objectives
  • Deliver high-quality education in core and emerging areas of Electronics and Communication Engineering to produce competent, industry-ready graduates.
  • Promote cutting-edge research and innovation to address societal, industrial, and environmental challenges.
  • Foster interdisciplinary learning and collaboration to develop advanced products, sustainable systems, and start-up ventures.
  • Develop professional, ethical, and leadership skills among students to excel in diverse global work environments.
  • Engage with industry and community through consultancy, skill development, and technology transfer for regional and national development.
